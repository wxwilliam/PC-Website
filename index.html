About Us


Founded in early 2019, The Ultima PC Parts.inc is well established as one of the best, most reliable online aftermarket retailers in the business. Catering to the needs of all late-model PC parts and enthusiasts, we sell the hottest products at the best prices you can find. Our staff offers top-notch customer service, both over the phone and in person at our 234,032+ sq.ft. warehouse located just 50 minutes outside of London, ON.
